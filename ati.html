NAME
       ati - ATI video driver

SYNOPSIS
       Section "Device"
         Identifier "devname"
         Driver "ati"
         ...
       EndSection

DESCRIPTION
       ati  is  an Xorg wrapper driver for ATI video cards.  It autodetects whether your hardware has a Radeon, Rage 128, or Mach64
       or earlier class of chipset, and loads the radeon(4), r128(4), or mach64 driver as appropriate.

SUPPORTED HARDWARE
       The ati driver supports Radeon, Rage 128, and Mach64 and earlier chipsets by loading those drivers.  See those manpages  for
       specific cards supported.
