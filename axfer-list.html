NAME
       axfer-list - dump lists of available sound devices and nodes to transfer audio data frame.

SYNOPSIS
       axfer list direction target

       direction = capture | playback

       target = device | pcm

DESCRIPTION
       The  list  subcommand  of  axfer  dumps lists of available nodes to transfer audio data frame. At present, the subcommand is
       helpful just for libasound backend of transfer subcommand.

OPTIONS
   Direction
       capture
              Operates for capture transmission.

       playback
              Operates for playback transmission.

   Target
       device Dumps a list of all soundcards and digital audio devices available in libasound backend for tranfer subcommand.

       pcm    Dumps a list of all PCM nodes available in alsa-lib configuration space in libasound backend for transfer subcommand.

COMPATIBILITY TO APLAY
       Options of -l , --list-devices are handled as device operation. Options of -L , --list-pcms are handled as pcm operation.
